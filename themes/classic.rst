Classic is the original SEO Panel theme that ships with every installation. It uses a clean blue and white colour scheme with a straightforward layout that puts all tools and data front and centre. It is lightweight, fully tested with every SEO Panel update, and compatible with all plugins.

**Colour scheme:** Blue navigation and headers, white content area, grey accents.

**Best for:** Users who prefer a minimal, familiar interface and maximum compatibility.

**Author:** Geo Varghese / SEO Panel Team

**Price:** Free — included with SEO Panel

This theme is already installed and active on a fresh SEO Panel installation. No download or setup is required unless you have switched to another theme and wish to return to Classic.

**To activate Classic:**

1. Go to **Admin Panel → Themes Manager**
2. Find **Classic** in the theme list
3. Click **Activate**
