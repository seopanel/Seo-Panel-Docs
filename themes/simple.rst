Simple is a minimal SEO Panel theme focused on clarity and usability. It strips away decoration in favour of a clean, uncluttered layout that lets you focus on your SEO data and tools without visual distraction. It is lightweight and fast-loading.

**Colour scheme:** Neutral greys and whites with minimal colour accents.

**Best for:** Users who prefer a distraction-free interface or who run SEO Panel on lower-powered servers where minimal CSS overhead matters.

**Author:** Geo Varghese / SEO Panel Team

**Price:** Free — included with SEO Panel

Simple ships with every SEO Panel installation alongside Classic. No download required.

**To activate Simple:**

1. Go to **Admin Panel → Themes Manager**
2. Find **Simple** in the theme list
3. Click **Activate**
