Themes
======

Themes control the visual appearance of your SEO Panel installation. Switch between themes without affecting your data or settings.

**Installing a theme:** Go to **Admin Panel → Themes Manager**, click **Install New Theme**, upload the downloaded theme zip file, and click **Activate** once installed.

**Upgrading a theme:** Download the latest version from seopanel.org, then go to **Admin Panel → Themes Manager** and select **Upgrade** from the theme's action menu.
